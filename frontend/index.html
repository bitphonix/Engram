Capture any AI conversation from Claude.ai, ChatGPT, or Gemini with one click. Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar — then click it on any AI page to capture.

← drag this to your bookmarks bar

How to use: 1. Drag the orange button above to your browser bookmarks bar
2. Open any AI conversation in Claude.ai, ChatGPT, or Gemini
3. Click the bookmark — Engram captures the page and extracts decisions
4. A popup confirms how many decisions and counterfactuals were saved
